About Us

We develop and market products and services that help people achieve optimal metabolic control ...because time is all that matters

Our customers use our proprietary technologies that predict the effects of food, physical activity, stress, and medication, which allows you to anticipate the best possible way to tweak what you normally do anyway, in order to achieve the energy level that you desire and fits your lifestyle. We patented mathematical models that predict individual outcomes in real time for what you will do next - as opposed to the traditional, population-based approaches that manage health and wellness through after-the-fact corrections.

We developed applications for weight loss, diabetes, physical exercise, and more, which will be released soon. It is a breakthrough approach to metabolic control that helps people improve their wellness by optimizing their nutrition, physical activity, and more, proactively, in real time, which we believe will lead to a new the gold standard for continuous improvement of outcomes in wellness and when managing many types of medical conditions.

Our solutions make use of the concept that feed-forward control is central to life and that evolution is, mathematically, the pursuit of hyperstability for organisms in order to evermore embed their natural environment. Thus, we are the first to recognize that the input dynamics impact on the stability of all living things, that is, life itself.

The company's work in mathematical modeling started back in 1992, when the founders' sons were diagnosed with type 1 diabetes, and naturally our work was aimed at improving blood glucose control. The clinical approach was inspired by Howorka's Functional Insulin Treatment, and Oaxman's Total Available Glucose, and was validated by proof of concept, human subjects tests at Duke University Medical Center, which provided the data that led to the development of our patented methodology.

Capable of becoming ubiquitous because of today's rapidly developing computing and communications environment, our unique, patented technology can revolutionize many clinical areas and build a commercial powerhouse, by applying our predictive models to a number of diseases and conditions that affect how we live in health, or when managing any medical condition, because glucose is the fuel of life and knowing the outcomes of how it will affect your body is the key component to a better life style.

AUREL PORUMBESCU, PH.D

C.E.O.

Aurel Porumbescu, Ph.D., achieved breakthroughs throughout his 30-year career in the management and growth of medical device companies, with emphasis on business development and high-tech: five years as Corporate VP with Invacare Corp. and 25 at Array Vision Engineering / LivProactiv. His technical career includes 15 years of advanced electro optics and nuclear spectroscopy work with Corning, Schlumberger, ITT, and Varian - developing unique materials and military and aerospace devices and systems. He authored numerous scientific and technological breakthroughs and is a specialist in Medical Device Manufacturing, He holds Ph.D. and M.S. degrees in EE/System Science from Polytechnic University of Bucharest and from Polytechnic University of New York, is a published author of scientific papers, poetry and fiction, and taught Statistics and Electrical Engineering at two U.S. Universities.
Dr. Porumbescu's track record includes three broad U.S. patents, he won funded research grants from NIH (NIDDK), DoD (BMDO), Sandia National Laboratories, and Lifescan Corp., and was invited to present his work at JDF and NASA's Technology Partnership Workshop on Non/Minimally Invasive Measurement of Biological Analytes.
Essential to our company's offerings is the theorem he proposed in his PhD thesis: "A system with feedforward loop(s) will be stable only if its structure contains elements that have dynamic characteristics that are 'close enough' to those of the feedforward loops' inputs," which is essential for this company's work. The corollary to his theorem is that in order to be stable - i.e., alive - the organism itself has evolved so that the dynamics of the inputs from the surrounding environment have become integral parts of its very structure. (The genome offers all the reference inputs necessary and proactive control is simplified because the inputs from the environment are measurable.)
